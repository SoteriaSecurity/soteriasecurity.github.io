About

Get to know the founders behind Soteria

<Our Foundation/>

Our team was formed by one question: How do we save people? Named after the Greek goddess for security, Soteria came to life from a group of students who wanted to address pressing issues, and after hearing about historical incidents like Uvalde, Sandy Hook, and Columbine, they decided to act. The team came together, ready to work towards making a difference and saving the lives of their fellow students.

Nathan Arega

Chief Technology Officer

Nathan Arega, a Python-based machine-learning expert, focuses on fine-tuning the code behind Soteria. His deep understanding of machine learning algorithms ensures the system runs flawlessly, detecting threats in real time with precision. Nathan’s work guarantees that Soteria’s software adapts and improves, enhancing the technology’s effectiveness as it responds to emerging security threats.

Omkar Bantanur

Chief Engineering Officer

Omkar Bantanur, with his experience in CPP-based machine learning, model training, and testing, has been instrumental in developing the software that powers Soteria. His expertise ensures the system's algorithms are continuously refined and tested to meet real-world challenges. Omkar’s role is crucial to the team, as he helps ensure that Soteria functions with high accuracy and efficiency.

Ekansh Dave

Chief Business Officer

Ekansh, one of the co-founders of Soteria, brought innovative thinking and meticulous planning to the team. With his strong background in finance, he laid the groundwork for the project's development, ensuring a solid foundation for future growth. His commitment to student safety and preventing tragic incidents fueled his drive to create a solution that could protect lives and make schools safer.

Aryan Singh

William Park

William's work on the website ensures that Soteria can effectively communicate its mission and provide essential services to its users.
